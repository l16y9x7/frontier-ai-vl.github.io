Overall Architecture

We adopt a fast perception & slow thinking mode for simple scenarios, and a reasoning & slow thinking mode (similar to R1) for complex or unknown scenarios. First, we generate task execution plans, then call MCP tool services to complete various subtasks, and finally use the large model to summarize results and output video analysis reports.

Our three contributions as follows:

  • Intelligent Model Routing: Dynamically selects the optimal model based on task complexity and requirements.
  • Fast Perception + Slow Thinking: Dual-mode processing for both real-time and complex analytical tasks.
  • Self-Planning + Self-Optimization: Autonomous task decomposition and continuous performance improvement.

Training Details

Framework & Foundation

  • Based on FUDOKI framework architecture
  • Foundation model: Qwen2.5-VL-7B
  • Optimized with discrete flow matching technology
  • Training data: 1M+ samples, 200GB+ total volume

Training Infrastructure

  • Training framework: LLaMAFactory + DeepSpeed
  • Precision: BF16 mixed precision training
  • Distributed training on 32x H100 GPUs
  • Duration: ~2 weeks, 3 epochs completion
